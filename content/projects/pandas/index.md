Research on implicit chain-of-thought reasoning in end-to-end Deep Research systems, with a focus on latent representation stability and effective-rank enhancement.

<!--more-->

During my internship at China Mobile Jiutian Research Institute, I studied how implicit reasoning tokens can better use complex process information without explicit reasoning traces. I designed and implemented an Effective Rank enhancement strategy to broaden latent-state representation space and improve reasoning behavior.
